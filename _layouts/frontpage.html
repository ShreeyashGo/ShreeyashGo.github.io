Shreeyash Gowaikar

Hi there! I'm a Computer Science Masters student at Georgia Tech specializing in Machine Learning, and working in the AI for Social Impact Team under Prof. Kai Wang.


Through my experiences at UdeM and Microsoft Research, I witnessed the immense potential AI holds to solve seemingly difficult but highly socially relevant questions.


However, seeing these systems in action also revealed a critical gap in their reliability. This drove me toward my current research focus on AI Robustness, where I am specifically investigating Fine-tuning Robustness to ensure models remain safe and stable as they adapt.
